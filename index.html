Welcome to ScholarTrack

Find the right scholarship. Track every application. Never miss a deadline.

ScholarTrack is a centralized platform that helps students discover scholarships they are eligible for, manage their applications from one dashboard, and track status updates in real time — replacing scattered spreadsheets, bookmark folders, and missed deadlines with a single, organized workflow.

Our Vision

To ensure that no deserving student misses an educational opportunity simply because they didn't know it existed or missed the application window.

Our Mission

To build an accessible, transparent, and intelligent scholarship discovery and tracking system that connects students with funding opportunities matched to their academic profile and eligibility.

Features

What you can do on ScholarTrack

  • AI-based scholarship matching based on academic profile and income criteria
  • Real-time application status tracking for every scholarship applied to
  • Deadline reminders via email and in-app notifications
  • Secure document vault for certificates, mark sheets, and income proofs
  • Eligibility checker before starting any application
  • Personalized dashboard showing saved, applied, and awarded scholarships

Services

How ScholarTrack supports your journey

  1. Scholarship Discovery — curated listings from government and private bodies
  2. Application Assistance — guided, step-by-step application filling
  3. Document Verification — automated checks before final submission
  4. Deadline Tracking — calendar sync for every active application
  5. Notification Alerts — SMS and email updates on status changes
  6. Application Status Updates — live tracking from submission to award
