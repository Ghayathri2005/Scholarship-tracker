Welcome to ScholarTrack

Find the right scholarship. Track every application. Never miss a deadline.

ScholarTrack is a centralized platform that helps students discover scholarships they are eligible for, manage their applications from one dashboard, and track status updates in real time — replacing scattered spreadsheets, bookmark folders, and missed deadlines with a single, organized workflow.

Our Vision

To ensure that no deserving student misses an educational opportunity simply because they didn't know it existed or missed the application window.

Our Mission

To build an accessible, transparent, and intelligent scholarship discovery and tracking system that connects students with funding opportunities matched to their academic profile and eligibility.
